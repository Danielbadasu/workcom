Who We Are

Paypot is a financial wellness company incorporated in London, Accra and Lagos, Nigeria that provides professional financial development solution for African Employees.

Our aim is to mediate social financial wellness and growth in average-income regions around Africa by providing salaried workers early access to earned wages before payday for spending or saving, with a goal of the alleviation of poverty.

Each Paypot user will have access to receive a payout amount based on their monthly needs. Paypot decided not to require other guarantee apart from the personal guarantee of each user agreeing to our terms of service. That means that no user will cosign / guarantee for the other. The Employer Partner guarantees the staff salary.
